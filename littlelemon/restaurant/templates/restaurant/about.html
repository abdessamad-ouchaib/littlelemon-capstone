About Us

Our story, our passion, our food.

Our Story

Little Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant. To craft an authentic Italian experience, the brothers moved to Chicago and opened Little Lemon restaurant in 2010.


The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek and Turkish cultures to develop a modern take on traditional dishes. Every day, we prepare our dishes fresh from locally sourced, seasonal ingredients.


Our Mission

We are committed to providing our customers with a unique dining experience that combines the best of Mediterranean cuisine with a warm, welcoming atmosphere. We believe that food is not just nourishment — it is a celebration of culture, family, and community.
